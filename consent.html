  3. What is the purpose of this research?

    You are invited to participate in a research study that aims to understand how different user interface designs can influence user performance for facility booking. This information sheet provides you with information about the research. Read the information below before deciding whether or not to take part.

  4. Who can participate in the research? What is the expected duration of my participation? What is the duration of this research?

    You can voluntarily participate in this research, if you:

    • have a good understanding of English
    • have never used NUS UTown* facility booking system before
    • are using desktop computer / laptop
    * "NUS UTown" is referring to the University Town located in National University of Singapore

    The expected duration of the participation is around 30 minutes.

    The duration of the research will be 2 months.

  5. What is the approximate number of research participants involved?

    There will be approximately 30 participants involved in this study.

  6. What will be done if I take part in this research study?

    If you have passed the screening questions, you will

    • answer pre-questionnaire
    • complete 12 series of room booking tasks
    • answer questionnaire at the end of each task
    • answer post-questionnaire

  7. How will my privacy and the confidentiality of my research records be protected?

    No Personally Identifiable Information (PII) will be collected during the research. As you interact with the facility booking user interface, the following data will be tracked and collected for further analysis:

    • Amazon MTurk Worker ID
    • IP Address
    • Browser Meta Info
      • Browser Type
      • Browser Version
      • Operating System
      • Screen Resolution
      • Flash Version
      • Java Support
      • User Agent
    • User Activity
      • Mouse Click
      • Task Duration
     

    The data can only be accessed by the Principal Investigator and co-investigators for academic and research purposes, and will not be disclosed to any third parties.

    All data collected will be kept in accordance to the Universityâ€™s Research Data Management Policy. Research data used in any publication will be kept for a minimum of 10 years before being discarded.

  8. What are the possible benefits to me and to others?

    By completing the survey successfully, there will be a renumeration of $1.85 per participant.

    There will be no renumeration if the participant fails to:

    • Meet the participation requirements stated in Section 4
    • Answer screening questions correctly
    • Complete the entire survey including all tasks or activities
     
    IMPORTANT

    You can only do this survey ONCE - any additional attempts beyond the first will not be paid. Your HIT will not be approved if multiple entries from the same Amazon MTurk Worker ID are detected.

  9. Can I refuse to participate in this research?

    Yes, you can. Your decision to participate is voluntary. You can withdraw from the research at any time without giving any reasons. By leaving the Qualtrics survey, all your data collected will be discarded. No penalty will be given.

  10. Whom should I call if I have any questions or problems?

    Please contact the Principal Investigator, Asst. Prof. Brian Lim through email brianlim@comp.nus.edu.sg for all research-related matters.
